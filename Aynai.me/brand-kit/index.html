A square cropped at one corner with a thin solder line marking the cut, paired with a JetBrains Mono wordmark whose only ornament is an Instrument Serif italic slash. The mark is the brand's only piece of decoration. Use it like a punctuation mark, not like a logo.

Construction · clear space · minimums

Clear space

The cap-height of the wordmark (the "a") is one unit. Reserve one unit on all four sides. Nothing else, including type, lives in that buffer.

Minimum sizes

Wordmark: 14px on screen, 8mm in print. Mark: 16px on screen, 6mm in print. Below those sizes the slash collapses and the mark stops reading. Use the favicon set instead.

§06 voice specifics · numbers · sentence case

Specifics, not adjectives.

AYN/AI sounds like the person who actually wrote the code, because that's who's writing the copy. Sentence case throughout. Real numbers. Short sentences. The reader's time matters more than the writer's cleverness.

do

Lead with the receipt.

"3,075 cold emails sent. 17.1% click rate. 14,070 leads in the database."

Open with the work that exists, not the work you'll do. Numbers earn the next sentence.

don't

Lead with the promise.

"We help ambitious founders unlock unprecedented growth through cutting-edge AI."

Adjective stack, no proof. Every AI agency website opens like this. Don't.

do

Be plain about scope.

"21 days to ship. $2,000 to start. One founder, no agency middlemen."

Numbers in the place of vague words. The reader can decide in 10 seconds.

don't

Hide behind the pitch.

"We tailor every engagement to your unique business needs and revenue goals."

Says nothing. Implies high price, slow timeline. Cut.

§07 motion motion is data, not decoration

Motion is data, not decoration.

Anything that moves on the page is reading something true. The mint dot pulses because the system is online. The scrubber moves because you're scrolling. Numbers tick once on first view because they're tabular. Nothing else animates.

Status pulse

The mint dot in the nav and on case statuses fades 1.0 → 0.4 → 1.0 across 3 seconds, shared across the page so all live indicators breathe in unison.

3.0s · cycle ease-in-out opacity only

Section scrubber

A 12px solder bead anchored to the left rule advances as you scroll, snapping to each §section. Indicates which section you're reading without a separate progress bar.

scroll-driven snap on section enter 180ms easing

Number count-up

Tabular numerals tick from 0 to value once, on first view, over 800ms. Tabular so the row never reflows. Plays exactly once, not on re-scroll.

800ms once per session tnum required

Hover

Cards lift to surface-2 (no shadow), buttons brighten by ~10%, the case-card arrow nudges right by 2px. All transitions 150ms, ease-out. No card flips, no parallax.

150ms cubic-bezier(.2,.7,.2,1) no shadows
