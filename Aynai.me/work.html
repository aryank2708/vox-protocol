01.
COLD EMAIL & OUTREACH B2B / India

cold email that actually lands.

Client: B2B Health Benefits Company, India. Service: Full Cold Email Automation System.

The Problem

Sales team manually emailing prospects one by one. No tracking, no personalization, no follow-up logic. Paying $500 a month for SaaS tools they didn't fully use. Results were inconsistent and the team was burning out on outreach.

What We Built

A fully automated cold email engine on n8n. 500 personalized emails a day across 10 sender accounts. AI assigned each lead one of 5 custom scripts based on industry and role. Every email was personalized by GPT-4o before sending. WhatsApp tracking layer flagged warm vs hot leads in real time. No CRM subscription needed. Total infra cost: $15 a month.

03.
AI VOICE AGENT Fintech / India

a voice bot that recovers crores.

Client: Fintech NBFC, India. Service: AI Voice Agent for Loan Recovery.

The Problem

A fintech NBFC had ₹20 crore sitting in overdue loans, completely idle. Their human calling team couldn't handle the volume. They needed a scalable way to reach thousands of borrowers, handle objections in natural Indian English, and hand off warm leads to human agents without dropping the conversation.

What We Built

A real-time AI voice bot built from scratch on Node.js. It calls borrowers, speaks in natural Hinglish, handles barge-ins, detects when to transfer to a human agent, and fires a full conversation summary to the agent before the handoff. Runs on Groq Llama 3.3 70B for sub-second responses and Sarvam AI for Indian language voice.
